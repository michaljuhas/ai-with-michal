# AI adoption levels

Most recruiters are not asking whether they should use AI anymore. The real question is how deeply AI is integrated into the way they work.
Here is a simple model you can use to assess where you are today.

## Level 1: Curious

You test tools occasionally, but AI is not yet part of your real workflow.

## Level 2: Prompting

You use AI chats for one-off help such as rewriting messages, summarizing profiles, or brainstorming search terms.

## Level 3: Systemizing

You save prompts, reuse context, and begin to build repeatable ways of working instead of improvising from scratch every time.

## Level 4: Automating

You connect steps together so AI helps move work forward without requiring full manual effort at every stage.

## Level 5: AI-native

You redesign the process itself around what AI makes possible, rather than simply layering AI on top of old habits.

## What to aim for before the workshop

You do not need to arrive as an expert. The best place to be is usually somewhere between Level 2 and Level 3:

- you have used AI enough to know the basics
- you can describe a few problems you want to solve
- you are ready to turn scattered experiments into more structured workflows

## Reflection prompt

Which level sounds closest to your current reality, and what is the one habit keeping you there?
