# What to expect at the workshop

The workshop is designed to be practical, focused, and immediately useful. The goal is not to overwhelm you with theory. The goal is to get you aligned on what strong AI-enabled recruiting looks like and how to move toward it.

## Session details

- **Date:** {WORKSHOP.displayDate}
- **Time:** {WORKSHOP.displayTime}
- **Format:** Live online workshop

## What we will focus on

- how AI changes recruiter leverage and expectations
- examples of modern recruiter workflows
- how to think about sourcing, screening, and reporting with AI support
- practical ways to move from experimentation to repeatable systems

## How to prepare

- review the pre-training modules in order
- note down the tasks where you feel most manual or slow
- come with at least one real recruiting challenge you want to improve
- be ready to think in workflows, not just prompts

## What you should leave with

- a clearer mental model for AI adoption in recruiting
- examples you can adapt to your own context
- stronger judgment around where AI helps most
- a more realistic path from manual work to higher-leverage systems

> If you are not fully "advanced" yet, that is fine. This workshop is designed to help you build a better foundation and direction.
